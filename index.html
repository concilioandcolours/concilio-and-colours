The Studio

Concilio, gathering — of design, print and colour.

It started with one 3D printer and a lot of curiosity. Now it's charms, keychains, night lamps, and one-off pieces — each one printed layer by layer, then hand-painted so no two finishes are quite identical. If you can describe it, there's a good chance we can print it, paint it, and get it to you.

How It Works

From idea to your doorstep

01

Tell us the idea

WhatsApp, email or the form below — share your quantity, colours and, if it's for an event, your date.

02

We quote & confirm

You'll get pricing and a colour plan based on size and detail before anything goes to print.

03

Print & hand-paint

Each piece is printed, then finished by hand — layer by layer, colour by colour.

04

It reaches you

Packed carefully and shipped, or ready for pickup — your call.
